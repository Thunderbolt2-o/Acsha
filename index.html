About C@NNECT 2021

C@nnect 2021 is a symposium of student's association of Departments of Electronics and communication, Electrical and Electronics, Computer and communication Engineering student’s association - 'ACSHA' of Amrita School of Engineering, Chennai. ACSHA is an archaic word which means ‘Let’s Move the World’. It is the acronym for ‘Association to learn Communicating Systems with Holistic Approach’. As the name propose our association’s core vision is to produce Electronics, Computer Communication & Electrical Engineers capable of generating knowledge with social responsibility.

Technology is the use of scientific knowledge for the welfare of all. It has become an integral part of industry and livelihood. Even a small pin and a big jetliner, all are the products of technology. And when it comes to the theme of the event its anything related to Electric vechicles and Batteries, Smart Batteries, Drones, AI and IOT for Electrical/ Electronics computing Communication systems and any other innovative topics are appreciated.

In the world full of fluctations, be someone's regulator!
